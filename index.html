↗
RISE
Pitch rises at the end. Typical in yes/no questions. Conveys doubt or request for confirmation. E.g. "Really↗?"
↘
FALL
Pitch falls at the end. Indicates statements, certainty and finality. Typical in declarative sentences. E.g. "I'm fine↘."
↗↘
RISE-FALL
Rises then falls. Expresses emphatic certainty, surprise or approval. E.g. "That's amazing↗↘!" Common in British English.
↘↗
FALL-RISE
Falls then rises. Indicates reservation, contrast or that the utterance continues. E.g. "I like it↘↗ but..."
⤴
FULL RISE
Marked rise (>8 st). Strong surprise, high emotion or emphatic rhetorical question.
